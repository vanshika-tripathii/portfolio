Devops

DevOps is a collaborative approach that integrates development and IT operations to enhance software delivery speed and quality. It emphasizes automation, continuous integration, and continuous delivery (CI/CD) to streamline workflows and reduce time to market. DevOps fosters a culture of collaboration, enabling teams to work together more effectively. By using tools like Docker, Jenkins, and Kubernetes, it ensures scalability, reliability, and improved deployment processes. Overall, DevOps bridges the gap between development and operations, promoting efficiency and innovation.
